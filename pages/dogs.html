Complete Dog Care Guide

Essential Dog Care Tips

Nutrition

  • Choose age-appropriate dog food
  • Maintain regular feeding schedule
  • Monitor portion sizes
  • Provide fresh water always

Exercise

  • Daily walks (30-60 minutes)
  • Interactive play sessions
  • Mental stimulation games
  • Breed-specific activities

Health Care

  • Regular vet check-ups
  • Vaccinations schedule
  • Dental care
  • Parasite prevention

Grooming

  • Regular brushing
  • Bathing as needed
  • Nail trimming
  • Ear cleaning

Training Tips

Basic Commands Every Dog Should Know

  • Sit, Stay, Come
  • Leave it
  • Walking on leash
  • House training

Remember: Positive reinforcement is key to successful training!
