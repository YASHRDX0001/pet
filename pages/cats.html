Complete Cat Care Guide

Essential Cat Care Tips

Nutrition

  • High-quality cat food
  • Wet and dry food balance
  • Fresh water always
  • Avoid toxic foods

Environment

  • Clean litter box daily
  • Provide scratching posts
  • Create vertical spaces
  • Safe play areas

Health Care

  • Regular vet visits
  • Vaccination schedule
  • Dental hygiene
  • Parasite prevention

Grooming

  • Regular brushing
  • Nail trimming
  • Ear cleaning
  • Occasional baths

Common Cat Behaviors

  • Purring and meowing
  • Body language
  • Territory marking
  • Social interaction
